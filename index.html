FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What type of projects do you take on?

I specialize in Shopify eCommerce and Webflow websites. This will consist of creating you a new store/website, store migrations, app integrations to make your life easier. Reach out to me and we can see how I can help you meet your needs.

How do you charge for projects?

I quote a price upfront so that you know what to expect. The price of your project will depend on the scope and requirements.

What is your hourly rate?

I only charge hourly for ongoing projects that need work regularly and is $35. One-time projects are charged upfront to keep things simple.

What is the typical timeline for a project?

This depends on the scope of the project. The best way to find out is to schedule a quick call with me.

What if I need help after the project?

I make sure to follow up with my clients after the project is complete. For any additional help post completion, we can work out an ongoing arrangement!
