The challenge

Cosmos Forge is doing affiliate marketing with a brand selling LED light strips. However, the default Shopify theme does not allow for an affiliate product page without the store selling the product themselves.

The solution

Using Shopify Liquid and custom front-end coding, I was able to create a product page for their affiliate products.

The result

Customers can now shop Cosmos Forge's own products and affiliate products. Cosmos Forge can now increase their revenue by cross marketing affiliate products.
